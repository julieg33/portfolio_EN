Projects

These are projects I did during my time at CESI.

First year of preparatory class 2019-2020

STRONGBOX

Simulation of a strongbox: 8 security guards are equipped with 8 electronic cards allowing them to open the strongbox. To pass the first authentication step, the card must be inserted and then a 4-digit code specific to each agent must be entered. Then, depending on the identity of the agent, an authentication method will be proposed, combining retinal scan (simulated by a function), additional code or a series of questions.
Skills: Arduino, electronics

ÝFANSI

Hephaestus, the Greek god, manages a particularly flourishing trade in legendary weapons and equipment. Hermes, the merchants god , is convinced that sales are less important than they could be. After many negotiations, Hermes convinces Hephaestus to let him manage the administrative part of his business. He then inherits all of his registers, i.e. 5 centuries of data. Only problem: these are totally messy!
Skills: BDD (modeling, MCD, implementation etc), SQL, UML

Second year of preparatory class 2020-2021

WORLDWIDE WEATHER WATCHER

The goal was to create a weather station to getthe parameters influencing on the formation of natural disasters, practical and very simple to use, controllable by a member of the crew. This on-board station has four operating modes: standard, maintenance, configuration, economy. It uses pressure, humidity and light sensors to obtain weather data and then write it to an SD card.
Skills: Arduino, Embedded system

LOGICIEL DE GESTION

A new company develops its information system. Its main activity is the online sale of electronic components. The goal is to achieve a solution that digitizes some of its business processes, namely the management of staff, customers, orders and stock (create, delete, add, modify) and the management of statistics (calculate average basket, turnover deal on a particular month, 10 most/least sold items, etc.).
Skills: C++, POO, Software architecture: Ihm – Services – Components, BDD, SQL, Test, GitHub

STAGE FINDER

In order to make the search for an internship easier and more practical, it would be necessary to have a website grouping together different internship offers, and which will make it possible to store the data of companies that have already taken on an intern, or are looking for one. The website will then be accessible by the administrator, the delegate, the promotion pilot and the student, who will all have different rights.
Skills: HTML, CSS, PHP, Javascript

First year of engineering cycle 2021-2022

PROGRAMMATION SYSTÈME

Our team was responsible for making a backup software respecting several constraints such as viewing the progress of a backup job, being able to act on it (pause/play/stop), keeping the backup information (date of execution, size, etc.) The software also had to be equipped with a remote interface to act on the work in progress (pause / play / stop)
Skills : C#, Software architecture, UML

BIGDATA & STOCKAGE

A medical group asked us to help them extract important information from their data. Indeed, they had a large amount of messy data, and asked us to find, for example, the number of deaths by region or the most active health professionals. For this, we had to take cognizance of their data, sort them and store them appropriately to carry out our analyses.
Skills : ETL/ELT, Hadoop, Talend, Cloudera, Hive, BigData, SQL, PowerBI

ALGORITHM

A transport agency called on us to optimize the delivery round. This tour had to be done under certain constraints such as a variation in traffic, passing through collection points or even a stock of gasoline to manage
Skills : TSP problem, Graph theory, Linear problem, Python

Second year internship (Solidnames, Bordeaux)

SN-SEO

Previously, Solidnames hand-designed websites for their clients which they tried to populate with optimized content using software such as 1.FR. This technique is very time consuming and the use of software can be expensive. So my mission was to develop a tool that, in one click, would do the work of an SEO optimized content writer. By specifying a theme and a few keywords, the program is able to create a website of several pages on this subject and should place this website in the first google results when these keywords are searched.
Skills: Typescript, Elasticsearch, Testing (Mocha and Chaï), Git

Third year internship (CATIE, Talence)

DATA-VIZ

Attached to the Algorithm and Data technological unit, my mission was to develop a visualization of data resulting from the running of a laser. This idea should eventually help the Amplitude experts to quickly analyze the state of the laser to carry out failure diagnosis. Amplitude is a company who made ultra-short lasers for scientific, medical and industrial applications. This collaboration, called “Laser 4.0”, aims to digitize the operation of lasers in order to develop a competitive advantage. Before, Amplitude experts have tools that are not very ergonomic to perform their analyses. These do not allow them to make effective analyzes quickly. Our goal will therefore be to provide them a tool that shows all the information they need to make their diagnoses in a clear and effective way.
Skills : Typescript, Elasticsearch, Test (Mocha et Chaï), Git
